About West-MEC

West-Mec is a public school district in which student can recieve career technical training which aims at preparing them to enter the workforce. Through hands-on experience, leadership training, inclass direction, and mentorship, west-mec programs provides students with the experience they need to work in a specialized career. While in the program, students are given the knowledge they need in order to pass a variety of industry certifications or licensure in a field of their choice, providing a pathway to success and career for their future. One of their programs; Law, Public Safety & Security is a two-year program which explores the basic skills used in patrol functions, defensive tactics, use of force and first aid.
