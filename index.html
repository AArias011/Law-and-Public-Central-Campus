About West-MEC

West-Mec is a public school district in which student can recieve career technical training which aims at preparing them to enter the workforce. Through hands-on experience, leadership training, inclass direction, and mentorship, west-mec programs provides students with the experience they need to work in a specialized career. While in the program, students are given the knowledge they need in order to pass a variety of industry certifications or licensure in a field of their choice, providing a pathway to success and career for their future.

Law and public Safety

One of the programs offered by West-MEC is Law and Public Safety, a two-year program which explores the basic skills used in patrol functions, defensive tactics, and the use of force. From learning first aid to one’s constitutional rights, the programs helps student in preparing for a future career in law enforcement. At the conclusion of the program, students are given the opportunity to demonstrate their knowledge on law enforcement by attempting a variety of industry certification examinations/ Further information about the program, pricing, dress code, and certifications can be located in the About page.
